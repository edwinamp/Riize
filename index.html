RIIZE

RIIZE (라이즈) is a combination of "Rise & Realize," which stands for a team that grows and realizes dreams together.

Seven members with their unique characteristics come together to create a "real-time odyssey" as a team. Based on their musical journey, RIIZE will present "Emotional Pop," an original genre expressing their own emotions and vibes to the audience.
